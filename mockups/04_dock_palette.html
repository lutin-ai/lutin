SNAC codec overview

SNAC (Scalable Neural Audio Codec) uses a hierarchical RVQ structure. At 24 kHz the encoder produces three codebooks at different temporal resolutions, which lets the decoder reconstruct waveforms with progressively finer detail.

01Coarse codes · 12 Hz, captures pitch + envelope
02Mid codes · 24 Hz, formants and tonal structure
03Fine codes · 48 Hz, transients and consonants
